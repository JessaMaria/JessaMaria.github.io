Delaware

Cristina Henríquez, Book of Unknown American (2014)

“We had all of our dreams pinned on this place, but the pin was thin and delicate and it was too soon to tell whether it was stronger than it looked or whether, in the end, it wasn;t going to hold much of anything at all” (32) Talking to Fito Angelino, the landlord, “'And here' I asked. 'Here is us! Venezuela, Puerto Rico, Guatemala, Nicaragua, Colombia, México, Panamá and Paraguay. We have it all.'' 'All in this building?'' 'You'll fit right in,”’ (36)
